# Privacy Policy – Block Puzzle Master

Block Puzzle Master does not collect, store, or share any personal user data.

All gameplay data, such as scores, settings, and preferences, are stored locally in the user's browser and are never transmitted to external servers.

This extension does not use analytics tools, tracking technologies, cookies, or third-party services.

No personal information is collected now or in the future.
